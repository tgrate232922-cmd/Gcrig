Our Process

How We Deliver Value

A rigorous, transparent process from initial engagement to ongoing monitoring — designed to maximise the quality and relevance of our intelligence.

01

Onboarding & Mandate Definition

We work with you to understand your investment objectives, risk appetite, and specific mandates before commencing any engagement. Every client relationship begins with a structured discovery process.

02

Data Collection & Analysis

Our AI engine aggregates 200+ data points per entity, combining quantitative models with qualitative analyst review. All data is sourced from verified, authoritative providers and updated continuously.

03

Rating / Report Delivery

Ratings and reports are delivered through our secure portal with full methodology documentation and analyst commentary. Every output includes confidence scoring and key risk factor summaries.

04

Ongoing Monitoring

We continuously monitor all rated entities and alert clients to material changes in credit profile, market conditions, or risk factors. Our relationship managers provide proactive guidance throughout.
