How It Works

Our Rating Methodology

A rigorous, four-stage pipeline from raw data to final rating — fully documented and independently auditable.

1

Data Ingestion

200+ data points per entity collected from verified sources, normalised and cleaned by our automated data pipeline before entering the analysis layer.

2

Feature Engineering

Raw data transformed into 85 engineered features across macroeconomic, political, ESG, and market dimensions — capturing non-linear relationships invisible to traditional models.

3

Model Ensemble

Seven ML models (Random Forest, XGBoost, LSTM, Transformer and others) vote on the rating outcome with confidence weighting, producing a robust consensus signal.

4

Rating Output

Final rating produced with confidence interval, outlook, key risk factors, and a full explainability report detailing the contribution of each data dimension.
