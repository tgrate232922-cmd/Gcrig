Network Overview

A Verified Marketplace for Capital

The Credit Rating Bridge (CRB) Network is GCRIG's proprietary institutional marketplace — a curated, KYC-verified ecosystem connecting global capital with emerging market opportunities.

Every member undergoes rigorous onboarding, continuous credit monitoring, and must meet our regulatory compliance standards. The result is a trusted network where deal execution happens with confidence.

Verified Member Onboarding

360° KYC/AML due diligence on every new member before network access is granted.

Continuous Credit Monitoring

Real-time AI scoring keeps all member credit profiles current and alerts on material changes.

Deal Flow Marketplace

A structured transaction pipeline matching investors with vetted emerging market opportunities.

Regulatory Compliance Framework

Multi-jurisdiction compliance standards built into every transaction and member relationship.

Process

How the CRB Network Works

A three-step journey from application to deal execution, built on rigorous verification and intelligent matching.

01

Member Vetting

Apply and undergo comprehensive KYC/AML verification and AI credit scoring. Our team conducts a 360° due diligence review before granting network access.

02

Network Integration

Gain access to the deal flow pipeline, counterparty matching engine, and real-time market intelligence — all powered by our AI advisory platform.

03

Deal Execution

Transact through our structured marketplace with full legal and compliance support. GCRIG provides ongoing monitoring and relationship management post-execution.

Member Types

Who Joins The Network

The CRB Network serves four distinct categories of institutional participant, each bringing unique capabilities and requirements to our marketplace.

🏛️

Sovereign Entities

Governments, central banks, and sovereign wealth funds seeking structured capital market access and independent credit intelligence for policy decisions.

🏦

Development Finance

DFIs, multilateral institutions, and development banks deploying concessional and blended finance across emerging market infrastructure and social projects.

🏢

Private Institutional

Pension funds, insurance companies, and family offices seeking emerging market diversification with robust due diligence and ongoing monitoring support.

🏗️

Corporate Issuers

Investment-grade corporates seeking international capital for growth and infrastructure projects, with GCRIG providing ratings, advisory, and investor introductions.
