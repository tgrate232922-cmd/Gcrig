About GCRIG

Building bridges between global capital and emerging market opportunities since 2009.

OUR STORY

Pioneering Global Cash Recycling

Founded in London in 2009, GCRIG — Global Cash Recycle Investment Group — was established to address a critical gap in global finance: the mismatch between surplus capital sitting idle in developed markets and high-yield investment opportunities in emerging economies.

Over 15+ years, we have built a world-class platform that channels surplus liquidity into structured investment opportunities across Africa, Asia, the Middle East, and Latin America, operating from 6+ global hubs with 180+ team members covering operations in 50+ countries.
