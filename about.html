Our Story

Built for the Future of Finance

GCRIG was founded with a singular mission: to democratise access to high-quality credit intelligence for emerging markets and close the information gap that has long held back institutional investment in these regions.

Who We Are

Global Cash Recycle Investment Group

GCRIG is an independent, AI-powered financial intelligence firm with a presence spanning key global financial hubs, including London, Dubai, Singapore, New York, and beyond.

We bridge the structural information gap between global institutional capital and high-potential emerging market opportunities. Our proprietary Cash Recycle Bridge (CRB) Network and AI advisory engine provide the transparency, data, and connectivity that institutional investors need to deploy capital with confidence.

GCRIG operates across the full investment lifecycle — from initial credit assessment and risk scoring to deal origination, due diligence support, and ongoing portfolio monitoring.

Our Journey

Milestones & Growth

From a small research boutique to a global credit intelligence platform — our journey reflects the growing demand for emerging market insight.

2009
Founded in London

GCRIG established as an independent credit research boutique focused on Sub-Saharan Africa. Initial team of 8 analysts with deep regional expertise.

2012
Middle East & North Africa Expansion

Opened Dubai regional hub and launched MENA coverage. Grew research coverage to 25 sovereign and 100+ corporate entities.

2015
Asia-Pacific Entry & Series A Funding

Singapore office opened. Raised Series A funding of $12M to accelerate technology development and geographic expansion into Southeast Asia.

2017
AI Platform Launch

Launched the first version of our proprietary AI advisory engine, integrating machine learning models for real-time credit scoring across all coverage universes.

2019
CRB Network Beta Launch

Introduced the Credit Rating Bridge Network concept with 50 founding members. Connected institutional investors directly with vetted emerging market deal flow.

2021
Global Scale — 340+ Partners

CRB Network surpassed 340 institutional members. Expanded Americas coverage with New York and São Paulo offices. Series B funding of $45M closed.

2023
ESG Intelligence Integration

Launched comprehensive ESG scoring module. Became the first emerging market intelligence provider to embed climate risk quantification into sovereign credit ratings.

2025
GCRIG 2.0 — AI Advisory Engine

Launched next-generation AI platform. Achieved $2.4B in assets under advisory. Recognised as the leading emerging market credit intelligence platform globally.
