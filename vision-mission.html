Purpose & Direction

Vision & Mission

Guided by a clear purpose: to transform how the world understands and accesses emerging market credit.

Our Purpose

What Drives Us

Two statements that define everything we build, every rating we publish, and every relationship we cultivate.

🌍

Our Vision

A world where every creditworthy emerging market entity — whether a sovereign nation, a growing corporation, or a development project — has equal access to global capital markets, judged on transparent, unbiased, data-driven merit.

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver the most accurate, transparent, and actionable credit intelligence on emerging markets, using proprietary AI technology and a global network of trusted relationships — enabling institutional investors to deploy capital with confidence and issuers to access the capital they deserve.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Six principles that guide every decision, every analysis, and every relationship at GCRIG.

🔬
Analytical Rigour

Every rating and recommendation is grounded in evidence, not intuition. We let the data speak — and we document every assumption we make.

🤝
Independence

We serve investors, not issuers. Our analysis is never compromised by commercial pressure or conflicts of interest that plague legacy rating agencies.

🌐
Inclusivity

We believe every creditworthy entity deserves access to capital, regardless of geography or the limitations of legacy rating systems designed for developed markets.

⚡
Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what AI can achieve in financial intelligence, investing relentlessly in our technology platform and research capabilities.

🛡️
Integrity

Transparency and honesty are non-negotiable in everything we do — in our ratings, our relationships, and our communications with clients and the public.

🌱
Sustainability

ESG principles are embedded in our analysis, not added as an afterthought. We believe sustainable finance is not just good ethics — it is good credit analysis.

2025–2030 Roadmap

Where We Are Going

Three ambitious targets that define our strategic direction for the next five years.

🌍

Expand to 100 Countries

Grow CRB Network coverage from 50 to 100 emerging market countries by 2028, expanding into Central Asia, Pacific Islands, and Eastern Europe — bringing rigorous credit intelligence to every frontier market.

Target: 2028
💰

$10B AUA by 2030

Scale assets under advisory to $10 billion through network growth, product expansion, and deepened institutional relationships — becoming the definitive platform for emerging market allocation.

Target: 2030
🏆

Set the Global Standard

Become the globally recognised independent standard for emerging market credit ratings, challenging the oligopoly of legacy rating agencies that have long underserved these markets.

Target: 2030
ESG Integration

Our Commitment to Sustainable Finance

ESG is not a product feature at GCRIG — it is embedded in our core analytical methodology and company culture.

🌿

Environmental

We quantify climate transition risk, physical climate risk, and carbon intensity in every sovereign and corporate rating. Climate change is a credit event — we treat it as such.

👥

Social

We champion inclusive finance, measuring social inequality, labour standards, and community impact. SME access to capital is central to our mission of democratising emerging markets.

🏛️

Governance

Anti-corruption scoring, board independence metrics, and transparency indices are core to our governance assessment framework — not optional add-ons.
